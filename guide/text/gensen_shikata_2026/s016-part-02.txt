[p2] II. Withholding Agents Under the withholding tax system, persons are obliged to withhold income tax and special income tax for
[p3] reconstruction subject to withholding, and pay it to the government are called "withholding agents." All payers of income subject to withholding are withholding agents, including schools, government agencies, and
[p4] even individuals and non-juridical associations or foundations, as well as companies.
[p5] However, salaries, etc., retirement allowances, etc. and remuneration/fees such as tax accountant s' fees that are paid by an individual who regularly employs no more than two domestic employees are not subject to
[p6] withholding income tax.
[p7] III. Place f or Payment of Withheld Income Tax and Special Income Tax for Reconstruction Income tax and special income tax for reconstruction withheld by a withholding agent shall be paid to the
[p8] competent tax office for the place of tax payment . The place of tax payment of the withheld tax shall, in principle, be the location of the office, establishment, etc., as it exists on the date income is paid, where the
[p9] income payer pays the income subject to withholding.
[p10] If the office, establishment, etc. where the income payer pays the income are relocated, the place of tax payment of withheld income tax and special income tax for reconstruction on the payment of the income
[p11] before the relocation shall be the location of the office, establishment, etc. after the relocation, which should be stated in a "Notification of Relocation of a Salary-Paying Office"（給与支払事務所等の移転届出書）.
