[p15] VII. Payment of Withheld Income Tax and Special Income Tax for Reconstruction 1 Due Date for Payment Income tax and special income tax for reconstruction withheld by a withholding agent shall be paid by the
[p16] 10th of the month following the month in which the income subject to withholding was paid.
[p17] If the due date is a Saturday, Sunday, or holiday, the next business day shall be the due date.
[p18] If payment is not made by the due date, the withholding agent may have to pay delinquent tax and additional tax for non-payment.
[p19] 2 Special Provision for Due Date Any withholding agent that pays salaries to fewer than 10 employees on a regular basis a nd submits an
[p20] "Application for Approval Made in Relation to the Special Provision for Due Dates f or Withholding Income Tax"（源泉所得税の納期の特例の承認に関する申請書）and obtains such approval may take advantage of a
[p21] special provision regarding the due dates for income tax and special income tax for reconstruction withheld from salaries, etc., retirement allowances, etc. and remuneration/fees paid to tax accountants, etc. Under this
[p22] arrangement, payment can be made twice a year as indicated below.
[p23] If no notice of approval or denial of the application has been received from the district director of the competent tax office by the last day of the month following the month in which the application was submitted,
[p24] the special provision shall be deemed to have been approved at the end of the month following the month in which the application was submitted and shall apply to tax payments made in the second month following the
[p25] month in which the application was submitted.
