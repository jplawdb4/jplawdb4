[p3] (Relatives qualified for withholding deduction)
[p4] A relative qualified for withholding deduction refers to a person who is either a dependent relative qualified for deduction or a specified relative whose total income is 1,000,000 yen or less.
[p5] (Note)
[p6] 1 If the only income of a specified relative is employment income, the total income shall be 1,000,000 yen or less if the yearly revenue from salary, etc. within a year is 1,650,000 yen or less
[p7] 2 It is not possible for both dependents to mutually qualify for exemption for specified relatives or qualify for exemption by designating specified relatives who is eligible for exemption for specified relatives.

[p17] Persons registered as Grade 1 or Grade 2 in this disabled persons category are persons with special disabilities.

[p28] (Widows)
[p29] The person herself who receives payments of salary, etc. and who falls under one of the following (Excluding people who fall under "Single parents" on the next page).
[p30] (1) A person who has divorced from her husband and has not since remarried and who falls under all of the following requirements.
[p31] (a) A person who has (a) dependent(s).
[p32] (b) A person whose total amount of income is 5,000,000 yen or less.
[p33] (c) A person who does not have a person who is conceived to be in a de facto marital relationship with that person.
[p34] (2) A person whose husband is dead and who has not since remarried or whose husband is not known to be living or dead and who falls under all of the following requirements.
[p35] (a) A person whose total amount of income is 5,000,000 yen or less.
[p36] (b) A person who does not have a person who is conceived to be in a de facto marital relationship with
[p37] [page 23]
