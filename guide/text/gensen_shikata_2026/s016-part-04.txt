[p15] 2. Procedure for Withholding from Employment Income The process of withholding from employment income paid to residents is broadly divided into the process of
[p16] withholding from monthly (daily) salary and bonuses, etc. , and the year-end adjustment process at the end of the year. The key points to consider here are: (1) the scope of taxable employment income, (2) the
[p17] requirements for the application of various deductions, such as exemptions for spouses and dependents, and (3) how to use the tax amount table and the methods for specific tax amount calculations.
[p18] (Note) The definitions of resident and non-resident are unrelated to nationality or residence (visa) status and it is determined by considering such factors as whether having a domicile in Japan or whether
[p19] continuously having a residence in Japan for 1 year or more. In each of the following cases, it shall be handled as below.
[p20] 1 If a person who is to reside in Japan has an occupation normally requiring a continuous residence in Japan for one year or more, the person is presumed to have a domicile in Japan.
[p21] 2 If a person who is to reside outside Japan has an occupation normally requiring a continuous residence outside Japan for one year or more, the person is presumed not to have a domicile in Japan.
[p22] * In this brochure, "the Tax Amount Table" means "the Withholding Tax Amount Table for Employment Income" (monthly table and daily table) and "the Table for Calculating Withholding Tax Amount for Bonuses".
[p23] I. Outline of Withholding Procedure The employment income withholding process and the tax amount table and application form used for withholding are as outlined below.
[p24] The National Tax Agency (hereinafter the "NTA") has established a special page in its website "Withholding Tax Agents"（源泉徴収義務者の方）, in Japanese only（https://www.nta.go.jp/users/gensen/index.htm） in
[p25] order to provide the latest information to withholding tax agents.
[p26] The special page contains information necessary for withholding procedure, including various forms such as "the Tax Amount Table ", "Application for (Change in) Exemption for Dependents of Employment Income
[p27] Earner", various kinds of guides and brochures such as the "Gensen choshu no aramashi"（源泉徴収のあらま し）, in Japanese only, and Q&As on withholding tax. We recommend you to visit the page in our website.
[p28] You can easily access the special page by clicking "Withholding Tax Agents" on the bottom right of the top page of the NTA website.
