[p44] 1. Outline of the Withholding Tax System I. Significance of the Withholding Tax System The income tax system is founded on the principle of the "self-assessment system," whereby an income earner
[p45] spontaneously calculates his/her income for the year and the corresponding amount of tax by submitting a return.
[p46] For certain types of income, the payer of the income is required to pay income tax withheld at the time the income is paid under the "withholding tax system."
[p47] Under the withholding tax system, (1) a payer of certain types of income, such as salary, interest, dividends and tax accountants' fees, (2) calculates the amount of income tax payable pursuant to prescribed methods at
[p48] the time the income is paid, and (3) withholds the amount of income tax from the income payment and pays it to the government.
[p49] With regard to special income tax for reconstruction, the withholding tax system has been adopted for the income subject to income tax withholding among the income arising between January 1, 2013 and December
[p50] 31, 2037, in which special income tax for reconstruction is withheld when withholding income tax and is paid with the income tax withheld.
[p51] Income tax and special income tax for reconstruction withheld under this system is settled through a year-end
[p52] adjustment or by filing a final tax return for the year, except for forms of income such as income from interest
